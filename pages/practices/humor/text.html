Humor practice

1

Think about one of your flaws, human weaknesses or painful places. Remember a certain trait of yours that actually seems funny when you look at it in perspective. By being able to laugh at yourself, you allow others to feel closer to you and encourage them to accept their own flaws, weaknesses and painful places.

2

Laugh at yourself. The next time you find yourself in a situation where you behave funny, say something funny or just be imperfect, laugh at yourself and joke about it. Humor is one of the best ways to end a conflict, especially when you can laugh at yourself and admit that you are reacting too emotionally or unreasonably.

3

Laugh at life. The next time you have unforeseen circumstances, try to find something funny in this situation instead of getting angry or resentful. You will notice how a cheerful mood helps others to relax and often smooth the situation. Similarly, when you encounter paradoxical cases in everyday life, try to perceive them with humor.
