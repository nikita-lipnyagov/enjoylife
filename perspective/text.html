Perspective practice

1

Think about the problem or situation you are facing.

2

Describe your problem as if it were happening to someone else. Use your name, not the pronouns "I", "me" or "mine".

3

Imagine what this problem would look like in a week, a year, or even ten years.

Will this question continue to affect you?

Will you even remember it?

What will this experience teach you?

4

Take a look at your life from a universal perspective. Look at your fears and frustrations from a different angle. Make sure all participants have the same value and deserve love and respect. Then ask yourself what will benefit everyone.
