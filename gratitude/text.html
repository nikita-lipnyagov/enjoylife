Forgiveness practice

1

Close your eyes and remember the three things you are dressed for today. It can be anything: the kindness and generosity of a friend, plenty of food, the warmth of the sun or the beauty of the night sky. Try to remember as accurately as possible the things for which you are grateful.

2

Write these three things in your diary. This exercise can be performed mentally, but, as experiments show, over time, maintaining such lists will have a positive effect on your physical and emotional state. Each time you make a new record, try to point out three different things. Diversity is the key to keeping an effective diary of gratitude.
