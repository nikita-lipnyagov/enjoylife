Forgiveness practice

1

Sit comfortably.

2

Take a few long breaths through your nose, and then perform a breathing meditation lasting one to two minutes.

3

Think of someone you love very much: a family, a friend, or even a pet. Try to imagine his face and feel his presence. Notice how your heart feels when you think of it.

4

Accept all the emotions that you have. If you feel warmth, tenderness and love, focus on those feelings. Otherwise, just think of the person dear to you.

5

Say the following phrases:

"I want you to be free from suffering"

"I wish you health"

"I wish you happiness"

"I wish you to find peace and joy"

6

Inhale, and as you exhale, imagine a warm light shining in the center of your heart. It conveys your love to your loved one and brings him peace and joy.

7

For a minute or a little longer, feel the joy of thinking about the happiness of a loved one.

8

Remember the difficult period in this person's life.

9

Notice how it is to feel human pain.

Does your heart hurt?

Do you feel stomach discomfort?

Want to help?

Just notice these feelings and focus on them.

10

Say the following phrases:

"I want you to be free from suffering"

"I wish you health"

"I wish you happiness"

"I wish you to find peace and joy"

11

Imagine that a warm light comes out of the center of your heart and touches the person you are thinking of, alleviating their suffering. Finally, sincerely wish her freedom from suffering.

12

Remember a time when you experienced great hardships and sufferings - as a child or even now.

13

Put your hand on your heart and notice the feeling of warmth, tenderness and care for yourself.

14

Consider that, like all people, you want to be happy and free from suffering.

15

Say the following phrases:

"I want to be free from suffering"

"I wish myself health"

"I wish myself happiness"

"I want to find peace and joy"

16

Imagine a person who causes you neither sympathy nor antipathy. This could be someone you often see at work, in the gym or in the store and to whom you do not have strong positive or negative feelings.

17

Think about the fact that, like all people, this person wants to be happy and free from suffering.

18

Imagine that this person has faced suffering - he/she has a conflict with someone close or he/she is experiencing despair and grief. Allow your heart to feel the warmth, tenderness and care of this person, as well as the desire to help her.

19

Say the following phrases:

"I want you to be free from suffering"

"I wish you health"

"I wish you happiness"

"I wish you to find peace and joy"

20

Consider that every person on Earth has a fundamental desire to be happy and free from suffering.

21

Fill your heart with the desire that everyone wants to be free from suffering, perhaps even someone with whom you have a difficult relationship. Repeat the following phrases:

"Let all living beings be free from suffering"

"Let all living beings be healthy"

"May all happy beings be happy"

"May all living beings find peace and joy"

22

Allow feelings of compassion and excitement to fill your heart, feel warmth, tenderness and care. Spread this compassion to the world.
